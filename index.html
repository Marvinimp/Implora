Who We Are

Implora is dedicated to helping factories and machine-heavy industries in Germany safeguard their operational know-how. We specialise in capturing the expertise of your senior blue-collar workforce, making sure essential, often undocumented, knowledge stays with your business – not just with your retirees.

The Problem

As experienced employees approach retirement, decades of practical knowledge are at risk of leaving your company. This knowledge is often undocumented, specialised, and essential for smooth operations – making its loss a critical risk for industrial companies.

The Implora Solution

Our process ensures that your critical knowledge is identified, structured, and transferred to the next generation of workers. With Implora, you’ll:

  • Reduce operational risks and downtime
  • Build a resilient, future-ready workforce
  • Retain competitive advantage by securing proprietary know-how
